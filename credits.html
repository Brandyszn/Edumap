This University Directory website was built by Wakhile Brian Malinga, with assistance from STEM mentors to help Eswatini students discover educational opportunities both locally and internationally.

About This Project

The University Directory is designed specifically for students from Eswatini, providing comprehensive information about:

  • Local universities and educational institutions
  • Regional opportunities in Southern Africa
  • International scholarship programs
  • Application tips and guidance
  • Cost calculators for education planning

Mission

To empower Eswatini youth with accessible information about higher education opportunities, making quality education more attainable for all students regardless of their financial background.
